About

ISTE

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in India with the motto of career development and personality development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System. One of the major objectives of ISTE is to assist and contribute in the development of top quality professional engineers. ISTE Students Chapter was inaugurated to actively participate the student community in ISTE activities and to provide a common platform for students to exhibit their talent and assist them in their career development and to get excellent positions in the engineering industry.
